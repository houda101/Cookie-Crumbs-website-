Lil Trivia

Where did cookies come from? 🤔

Originally cookies served only the purpose of testing the oven in the kitchen by baking a small portion of the cake batter before baking the whole cake. Cookies appeared in the 7th century AD, Persia and its name is derived from the Dutch word 'Koekje' meaning 'small cake'.

How did Ghaniya start baking?👀

During highschool Ghaniya baked cookies for her besties on their birthdays and their positive honest feedback motivated her to make more then her family started encouraging her to bake more cookies since they were simply a delight and thus Cookie Crumbs was born🥰.
